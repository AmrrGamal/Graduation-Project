About Aidentify

Revolutionizing Dental Diagnostics with AI

Our Story

Aidentify was founded in 2023 by a team of dental professionals and AI experts who recognized the potential of artificial intelligence in revolutionizing dental diagnostics. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make dental care more accurate, efficient, and accessible.

From our initial launch, we've grown to serve thousands of dental professionals worldwide. Our platform has processed millions of dental X-rays, helping to improve diagnostic accuracy and patient care across the globe.

We continue to innovate and expand our capabilities, working towards our goal of becoming the global leader in AI-powered dental diagnostics. Our commitment to excellence and innovation drives us forward.

Our Mission & Values

To empower dental professionals with cutting-edge AI technology, improving diagnostic accuracy and patient care while making dental education more accessible and effective.

To become the global leader in AI-powered dental diagnostics, setting new standards in dental care and education through innovative technology.

Innovation, accuracy, accessibility, and continuous improvement drive everything we do. We're committed to advancing dental care through technology while maintaining the highest standards of quality and ethics.
